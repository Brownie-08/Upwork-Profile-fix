Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how LusitoHub ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our platform. By accessing or using LusitoHub, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, enhance functionality, and improve user experience.

Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.
Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device until deleted or expired.
First-Party Cookies: Set directly by LusitoHub.
Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services integrated into our platform.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the operation of our platform. They enable core functionalities such as account login, secure browsing, and payment processing.

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience by remembering preferences and settings, such as language selection and saved login information.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your activity to deliver personalized advertisements and promotional content based on your interests.

Analytical Cookies

We use these cookies to collect data on how users interact with our platform. This helps us understand user behavior and improve our services.

3. How We Use Cookies

LusitoHub uses cookies to:

  • Improve platform performance and functionality.
  • Personalize your user experience.
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns.
  • Deliver targeted advertisements and promotional offers.
  • Ensure platform security and fraud prevention.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete stored cookies.
  • Block third-party cookies.
  • Disable all cookies.

Please note that disabling cookies may impact your ability to use certain features of LusitoHub.
